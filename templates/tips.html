🛠️ Smart Construction Tips

1. Plan Your Budget Before Building 🧮

Always estimate your total budget in advance. Consider land, labor, materials, permits, and interiors.

2. Choose the Right Material for Your Climate 🌦

Materials like clay bricks, AAC blocks, or fly ash are better suited for different regions. Don't just go by price.

3. Avoid Last Minute Design Changes 🧱

Design changes during construction increase cost and time. Finalize designs early with your architect.

4. Use Energy Efficient Fixtures 💡

Install solar panels, LED lighting, and energy-saving appliances to reduce electricity bills in the long term.

5. Verify Contractor Credentials 👷‍♂️

Check for registered licenses, past work, and ask for reviews before finalizing your contractor.
